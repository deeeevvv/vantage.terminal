Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Vantage Terminal collects, uses, and protects information. We believe in a "Privacy First" approach.

1. Data Collection

We operate with a client-side architecture, meaning we do not store your personal data on our servers.

  • Local Storage: Your preferences, such as Dark Mode settings and your Asset Watchlist, are stored locally on your own device using browser `localStorage`.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your physical location or your trading patterns.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide real-time functionality, our application connects to third-party APIs. When these requests are made, your IP address may be visible to these providers solely for connection purposes:

  • ExchangeRate-API: Used to fetch live currency conversion rates.
  • Yahoo Finance / RSS2JSON: Used to fetch the Macro Intelligence news feed.
  • Pollinations.ai: Used to generate AI images for news articles.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or marketing pixels. The only data persistence is strictly functional (saving your theme preference and watchlist) and remains entirely on your client device.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please contact us at privacy@vantageterminal.com.
